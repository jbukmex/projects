Undercover Tsarist Agent

Jacob Batchelor

Anton Solokov (You) 1905

Intro

It's early march 1905, you are an Undercover Tsarist Agent named Anton, you're hanging out with your friends, (peasants); Alexei, Dimitri and Igor. You're job is to discover if any of them have come under the influence of revolutionary parties with extreme views. You have the evening to find out and may only use the questions given, good luck.

Oh, one last thing, you have the ability to call other agents at any time, but they will take two slides to arrive, Удачи, and mey Perun guide you to your goal!


Begin!

Удачи

Russians usually say simply "Удачи". This word comes from a longer sentence: "Желаю тебе удачи," (I wish you luck). This is the reason why we use the word "удача"!
