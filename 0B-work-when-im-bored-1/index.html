25/02/21 - Emotion: Nintendo related boredom.

It's thursday 25th February 2021 and the first Nintendo Direct since September 2019 was a week ago. We recieved no new information related to the sequal to 'The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild.' We did recieve news that 'The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword' is set to release on July 16th, which - of course - is great news.
Hopes were high for the Zelda 35th Anniversary last Sunday, but once again, no news. We've had no news whatsoever - besides that fact that there is no news - related to the sequal trailer in June 2019.

The video above is seriously the only thing that we've had for nearly two years! I know, it's only been nearly two years, but it's still annoying that we haven't had any comments from nintendo, beside something along the lines of "We can't give any news related to the sequel of Breath of the Wild, but please be patient, news is coming this year."
I don't want to sound impatient, but a line like: "The sequel to Breath of the Wild is still in production" would be satisfactory.
Anyway, on a brighter note, we have some dates to look forward to for some possible news.

  • Wednesday 3rd March 2021 - This, I think is one of the most likely dates, is the mark of the four year anniversary of Breath of the Wild. I honestly can't wait, and I do think that Nintendo will release something. Hopefully.
  • Wednesday 31st March 2021 - This is the end of the time limit to purchase the Mario 3D All Stars Game from Mario's 35th Anniversary. This could mean some news related BotW 2 since Mario's time would be over, and Zelda's would start, but Pokemon's 25th Anniversary is this year as well, so it's unknown.
  • Friday July 16th 2021 - Finally, This is the day Skyward Sword and the Zelda Joy-cons are released, to go with the occasion, it's possible that Nintendo will release some Botw 2 news.

Anyway, I think that's all I have to say, hope you enjoyed some usesless opinion and some useful dates, goodbye!
